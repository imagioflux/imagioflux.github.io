Prompt Guide



Craft prompts using clear, concise sentences with commas to separate details for better clarity. Be specific and focus on the main object or subject, ensuring supporting details complement rather than overshadow it. Avoid overloading the prompt with excessive information, maintain logical flow, and use adjectives sparingly to enhance descriptions without causing confusion. Ensure consistency by avoiding contradictions, provide context when needed, and refine prompts iteratively to achieve the best results.
